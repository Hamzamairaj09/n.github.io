Artciti (Karachi, Pakistan)


Artciti, established in January 2014, is recognized as Pakistan's first online art gallery. Based in Karachi, it has rapidly become a prominent platform for promoting Pakistani art and artists globally. In its initial six years, Artciti organized over 55 exhibitions, showcasing works from both renowned and emerging Pakistani artists. The gallery has also featured international artists, providing local audiences with access to diverse artistic expressions. Artciti's mission is to promote Pakistani talent in art across the globe. The gallery's online presence pioneers in showcasing indigenous artists' work worldwide, making strides to open a window to the world for talented artists of Pakistan. TRIPADVISOR INSTAGRAM The gallery is located at Plot # 36-C, 2nd Floor, Bukhari Commercial Lane-13, Khayaban-e-Shujaat, Phase VI, DHA, Karachi, Pakistan. Artciti continues to be a significant contributor to Pakistan's art scene, fostering appreciation for the country's rich cultural heritage and contemporary artistic endeavors.
